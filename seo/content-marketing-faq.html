Writer FAQs

Q: What tests does a writer have to go through?

To be able to accept jobs from any category, a writer must give a topic proficiency test first. The test requires the writer to submit a 150 word write-up on a randomly generated topic.In addition, a writer can give three references of past work he/she has done and has been published on the internet.
A writer can give the topic proficiency test for as many topics he/she wishes, but can give each test only once.

Q: How many types of pay-grades are there?

There are four types of pay-grades, namely:-

  1. WhitePanda Novice
  2. WhitePanda Journeyman
  3. WhitePanda Veteran
  4. WhitePanda Expert

Novice writers have access only to bronze assignments, Journeymen have access to bronze and silver, Veterans to all but platinum jobs while experts have access to all jobs posted on WhitePanda.
A writer can belong to only one pay-grade.

Q: How is a writer’s pay-grade determined?

The write-up and the references (the proficiency test) determine the writer’s pay-grade in that topic.

Q: How many jobs can a writer work on at a time?

A writer may work on only one job at a given time.

Q: Can two writers work on the same job?

No. Jobs with guidelines shall be made available to the writers as soon as the job is posted by the client. Once a writer selects a job, it shall disappear from other writer's feeds.

Q: Can a writer’s account be terminated? In what cases?

A Writer’s account could be terminated due to the following reasons, solely at WhitePanda’s discretion.

  • Writer makes five late submissions.
  • Writer fails to submit a job altogether twice.
  • Any serious breach of the writer service agreement.

Q: How are writers promoted?

WhitePanda believes in rewarding consistency and good performance. Writer promotion is determined by a credit based system.

  • Good Editor reviews earn you credits.
  • Good Client reviews earn you credits.
  • Taking Jobs regularly earns you credits.
  • Submitting Jobs before the deadline also earn you credits.

Q: Can the writers be demoted? And how?

Yes, a writer can be demoted.

  • Bad Editor reviews reduce credits
  • Bad Client reviews reduce credits.
  • Missing submission deadlines reduce your credits. Five such offences will result in account deactivation.

Q: How can a writer earn credits?

WhitePanda uses a credit based system to judge a writer’s performance .On the accumulation of a sufficient number of credits, the writer becomes eligible for a promotion. Credits are added or deducted after each job based on the writer’s performance on that particular job. Credits may also be earned by taking jobs more frequently.

Q: How does a writer get his/her payment?

A Writer may view his/her account in the account section. The writer may choose to withdraw this amount at any time between 5pm and 11pm. We at the moment shall only be able to process requests over Rs 500.

Q: How much time is given to complete one job?

He/she has 48 hours to submit the job to WhitePanda.

Q: In how much time is a writer paid for a job?

If client deems a job satisfactory, then the fee shall be added to the writer’s account 72 hours after the job is submitted to the client.
